Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

CineDepth Pro ("the App") is developed by Weiyuan Kong ("we", "us", or "our"). This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal information directly. The App processes photos and depth data entirely on your device.

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect data independently:

  • Google AdMob – serves ads and may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to personalise ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google ML Kit – face detection runs on-device; no image data is sent to Google servers.

2. Permissions Used

  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_MEDIA_VISUAL_USER_SELECTED – to let you select photos from your gallery for editing. Images are processed locally and never uploaded.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 9 and below only) – to save edited photos to your device.
  • INTERNET – required by AdMob to load advertisements.

3. How We Use Information

We use your photos solely to apply the depth and lens blur effects you request. No photo or depth data leaves your device.

AdMob may use advertising identifiers to show relevant ads. You can opt out of personalised ads in your device settings under Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.

4. Data Retention

We do not retain any user data on our servers because we do not collect any.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
